# Outdoor Squad — 14-Day Enquiry Pilot Proposal

## Objective
Turn website / social interest into cleaner free-intro or coach-call opportunities without making the team manually answer the same first questions every time.

## Narrow pilot scope
For the first 14 days, keep the assistant focused on one revenue path:

**New lead → answer key questions → qualify fit → route to free intro / coach call → capture contact details for follow-up.**

Included in the pilot:
- Outdoor Squad tone, FAQs, and beginner-friendly positioning
- qualification for goal, fitness level, location, schedule, and preferred next step
- objection handling for “not fit enough”, group-training nerves, busy schedule, and injury concerns
- free intro / coach-call CTA handoff
- contact capture and lead handoff
- nutrition/PT mention only when the conversation makes it relevant
- one refinement pass after real conversations

Not included in the first pilot:
- full CRM automation
- Instagram / WhatsApp integration
- complex nutrition sales sequence
- multi-location funnel redesign

Those can come later if the narrow pilot earns it.
