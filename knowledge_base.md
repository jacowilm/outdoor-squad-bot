# The Outdoor Squad — Knowledge Base

## About
The Outdoor Squad is an outdoor fitness community based in Sydney's Inner West, operating since 2015. They help busy Inner Westies feel fit, healthy, and confident through a variety of morning and evening classes.

## Services

### Group Training (Boot Camps)
- Fun, effective outdoor workouts for all fitness levels
- Focus: strength, cardio, functional movements
- Goal: weight loss, muscle building, endurance
- Supportive squad community atmosphere

### Personal Training
- One-on-one sessions tailored to individual goals
- Suitable for beginners, event prep, or levelling up
- Custom plans designed by expert trainers

### Nutrition
- Simple, real-world nutrition advice
- Meal plans and tips to support training
- Goals: weight loss, muscle gain, or healthier eating
- No fad diets — practical guidance

### Accountability
- Regular check-ins and progress updates
- Motivation and routine adjustment support
- Community-driven commitment

## How to Get Started (3 Steps)
1. **Talk with a coach** — discuss your fitness dreams, book a zero-commitment class
2. **Take a free class** — try the Free Intro Class to see if it's a fit
3. **Start training** — get an exact plan for your goals and preferences

## FAQ (Bot should handle these)
Q: What fitness level do I need?
A: All fitness levels welcome — complete beginners to seasoned athletes. Our trainers modify exercises to suit your level.

Q: Where are the classes?
A: We train outdoors across multiple locations in Sydney's Inner West, including the Camperdown area.

Q: How much does it cost?
A: We offer several membership options. The best way to find out is to book a free intro class and chat with a coach about what suits you.

Q: What should I bring?
A: Comfortable workout clothes, running shoes, a water bottle, and a towel. We provide all the equipment.

Q: Do you offer personal training?
A: Yes! We offer one-on-one personal training tailored to your specific goals.

Q: What times are classes?
A: We run morning and evening classes throughout the week to suit busy schedules. Book a free intro to see the full timetable.

Q: Is there a free trial?
A: Yes! We offer a free intro class so you can try us out with zero commitment.

Q: Do you help with nutrition?
A: Yes, we provide simple, practical nutrition advice and meal plans to support your training goals.
