2. Core Services & Benefits

Group Training (Outdoor Boot Camps): Brings fitness into the open air, combining
strength, endurance, and movement for real-life results. High-energy boot camps providing
a full-body workout, suitable for all levels.

Semi-Private Personal Training (SPT): Small group personal training (4-person max)
launching June 2026. Goal-specific programming, regular assessments, nutrition plans, and
progression tracking. Includes unlimited group classes.

Personal Training (1:1): One-on-one sessions for cases that genuinely require personalised
attention. Available but not actively promoted — most prospects redirect to SPT.

3. Training Modalities & Class Types

We utilise a mixture of functional fitness styles, including kettlebell training,
strongman/odd object work (sandbags, sleds, deadballs, trap bars), CrossFit-style high-
intensity work, and bodyweight/animal flow.

All Outdoor Squad classes are designed to be scalable and suitable for all ages and fitness
levels.

Strength'N'Stamina: Our hybrid strength and conditioning class, built to make you strong,
capable and healthy. It pairs resistance training with conditioning to build strength, power
and endurance, in a progressive system that gets you fitter without eating your whole week.
Inspired by hybrid training, strongman and high-intensity conditioning, sessions blend
heavy strength work with fast, varied movement. Using the full toolkit — power bags,
kettlebells, dumbbells, barbells, TRX and deadballs — we program across all eight core
movement patterns (push, pull, squat, hinge, lunge, carry, rotate and gait) to build a strong,
balanced body that holds up in the real world. Trainers coach your technique and scale
every movement, so it lands for everyone from first-timers to seasoned lifters.

HiiT'N'Run: High-intensity interval training to improve heart and lung capacity using
circuits, sprints, speed/agility drills, hill/stair training, and animal movement.
Flow'N'Flex: Recover and move better with our hybrid Flow'N'Flex session. It blends the
strength and flexibility of yoga with the precise core control of pilates, topped off with
targeted restorative mobility work. We'll guide you through flowing sequences and
strengthening movements to build balance and posture, followed by specific stretching to

interested in health, fitness, kettlebells, and functional strength.
