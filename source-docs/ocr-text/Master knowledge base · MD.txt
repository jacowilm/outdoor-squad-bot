2. Core Services & Benefits

Group Training (Outdoor Boot Camps): Brings fitness into the open air, combining strength,
endurance, and movement for real-life results. High-energy boot camps providing a full-body
workout, suitable for all levels.

Semi-Private Personal Training (SPT): Small group personal training (4-person max)
launching June 2026. Goal-specific programming, regular assessments, nutrition plans, and
progression tracking. Includes unlimited group classes.

attention. Available but not actively promoted — most prospects redirect to SPT.

3. Training Modalities & Class Types

We utilise a mixture of functional fitness styles, including kettlebell training, strongman/odd
object work (sandbags, sleds, deadballs, trap bars), CrossFit-style high-intensity work, and
bodyweight/animal flow.

All Outdoor Squad classes are designed to be scalable and suitable for all ages and fitness levels.
Strength'N'Tone: Progressive resistance training system to improve functional strength and
power. Utilises power bags, kettlebells, dumbbells, TRX, bodyweight, medicine balls, and
deadballs.

HiiT'N'Run: High-intensity interval training to improve heart and lung capacity using circuits,
sprints, speed/agility drills, hill/stair training, and animal movement.

Power'N'Pilates: Dynamic sessions weaving together precise movement, mindful breathing, and
targeted strengthening to power up posture, core, and control.

Core'N'Sore: Focuses on stabilising, enduring, and strengthening core muscles using both
weighted and bodyweight movements, plus high-heart-rate finishers. Primary home for animal
flow work.

Yoga Squad: Energising sessions blending breathwork, mobility, and mindful movement
(flowing sequences and held postures) for strength, balance, and flexibility.

Outdoor Hyrox: Inspired by the functional fitness race. Alternates short running intervals with
eight core functional movement stations (push, pull, squat, hinge, lunge, carry, rotate, gait) using
sleds, power bags, and kettlebells.

Buff'N'Puff: A hybrid and Hyrox-style class seamlessly integrating resistance training and
cardiovascular exercises for a comprehensive, time-optimised workout.
