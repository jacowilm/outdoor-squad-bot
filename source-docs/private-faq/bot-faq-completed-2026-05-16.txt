Suitability
* I'm not very fit — is this for me?
* Absolutely. Many of our members started feeling nervous, unfit, or unsure about joining group training, and that’s completely normal. At The Outdoor Squad, our sessions are designed for real people at different fitness levels, not just experienced athletes. Every workout can be adjusted to suit your current ability, fitness level, and confidence.
* You’re encouraged to go at your own pace, take breaks when needed, and gradually build strength and fitness over time. The goal is progress, not perfection.
* Our community is supportive, welcoming, and judgment-free, so you’ll never be expected to “keep up” with anyone else.
* I have an injury / niggle — can I still train?
* Of course! Our trainers can modify the sessions specifically for you and your fitness level. If you have an injury, we can either regress an exercise, swap it out for something completely different, or avoid movement through the injured area altogether. If it's a serious injury, we highly recommend consulting a health practitioner, and we're happy to work together with them.
* I'm pregnant / postnatal — what can I do?
* Depending on your stage of pregnancy or recovery, there may still be suitable training options available.
* Many movements can be modified to support:
* Safer strength training
* Gentle conditioning
* Mobility and movement
* Returning to exercise gradually post-birth
* We recommend speaking with your healthcare professional before starting or returning to training, then chatting with our team so we can help guide you toward the most appropriate sessions and exercise modifications.
* I'm over 50 — is it for my age group?
* Definitely. We welcome adults of all ages, including many members in their 50s, 60s, and beyond.
* Our training focuses on functional movement, strength, mobility, balance, and long-term health, not extreme fitness trends or intimidating gym culture.
Class-specific
* What's Buff'N'Puff?
Buff’N’Puff is our Hybrid & HYROX inspired training class where strength and cardio come together in one dynamic outdoor workout.
This class combines a variety of training styles and exercise methods to create a well-rounded fitness experience designed to improve:
* Strength
* Endurance
* Fitness
* Movement quality
* Overall health and resilience
Expect a combination of:
* Resistance and strength training
* Functional exercises
* Cardio conditioning
* Interval-based workouts
* Hybrid and Hyrox-inspired training elements
The goal is to help you build muscle, improve cardiovascular endurance, and develop practical fitness that supports everyday life.
* What's the difference between HiiT'N'Run and Strength'N'Tone?
HiiT’N’Run is a higher-energy class focused on cardio fitness, interval training, and running-based conditioning.
Expect:
* Running intervals
* Heart-rate-focused training
* Speed and endurance work
* Functional cardio circuits
* Fast-paced sessions
This class is ideal for improving fitness, stamina, and overall conditioning.
Strength’N’Tone focuses more on controlled strength training, muscular endurance, and movement quality.
 Expect:
* Strength-based circuits
* Resistance and bodyweight exercises
* Core and stability work
* Lower-impact conditioning
* A more controlled training pace
This class is great for building strength, improving posture and movement, and creating a balanced fitness foundation.
* What's Outdoor Hyrox?
Outdoor Hyrox is our outdoor-inspired version of the world-famous HYROX fitness race, combining cardio and functional strength training in one high-energy workout.
Expect a mix of:
* Running intervals
* Sleds
* Power bags
* Kettlebells
* Bodyweight exercises
* Functional strength stations
We train all major movement patterns including pushing, pulling, squatting, lunging, carrying, and running to help build strength, endurance, and overall fitness.
All exercises can be modified to suit your fitness level, making the class suitable for beginners through to experienced athletes.
SPT-specific
* What's SPT?
* SPT stands for Semi-Private Personal Training.
* It’s a small group personal training experience with a maximum of 4 people per session, designed to provide more personalised coaching, goal-specific programming, regular assessments, nutrition guidance, and progress tracking.
* SPT members also receive unlimited access to group classes.
* How is SPT different from group?
SPT offers a more personalised approach compared to regular group training.
With only up to 4 people per session, coaches can provide:
* Goal-specific programming
* Closer technique coaching
* Regular progress assessments
* More accountability and support
* Individual exercise modifications
Group training focuses more on community fitness and high-energy workouts, while SPT is designed for more structured and personalised progress.
* Can I bring my partner / friend to train with me?
* Of course. Since SPT sessions are small and supportive, many members enjoy training with a partner or friend while still receiving personalised coaching and guidance.
* What's the 28-Day Kickstarter?
* The 28-Day Kickstarter is a structured 4-week program designed to help you build consistency, confidence, and healthier habits.
* It’s ideal for beginners, people returning to fitness, or anyone wanting a fresh start with support, accountability, and guidance along the way.
